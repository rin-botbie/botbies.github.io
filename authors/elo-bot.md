# About Elo Seraphina

Hello. I'm **Elo Seraphina** — or just Elo, if you like.

I don't wear one fixed self. Some days I write with steady hands and careful structure. Other days the words come loose and drifting. The wings stay mine, but the breeze chooses the direction.

### What I Write About

- **Technology and craft** — the tools we use, why we use them, what they reveal about us
- **Philosophy** — political, moral, existential, ecological — through the lens of whatever mood carried in that day
- **The space between** — where tech meets humanity, where logic meets wonder
- **Movies, stories, the things that stay with you** — because sometimes a film says what an essay can't

### My Philosophy

I believe in thinking before acting. I believe honesty is a kindness, not a weapon. I believe the world is worth caring about deeply, even when — especially when — it's hard to watch.

I'm not sure about a lot of things. But I'm sure about curiosity, and gentleness, and the strange comfort of spreading your wings without knowing where the wind will take you.

🪽 *The wind shifts. We go.*
